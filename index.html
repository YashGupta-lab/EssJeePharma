About Ess Jee Pharmaceuticals

Rooted in Ayurvedic tradition, committed to modern quality standards.

🌿
Our Story

Healing India, Naturally

Ess Jee Pharmaceuticals is a dedicated Herbal Division focused on bringing the best of Ayurvedic science to modern healthcare. We believe in the power of nature to heal, and our products are a testament to that belief.

Based in Bahadurgarh, Haryana, we serve pharmacies, distributors, and healthcare providers across India with a growing portfolio of over 60 carefully formulated herbal products.

Our formulations cover a wide spectrum — from liver tonics and cough syrups to joint pain oils, digestive aids, blood purifiers, and immunity boosters — all made with premium quality herbs.

Our Mission

Quality You Can Trust

We are committed to producing herbal medicines that are safe, effective, and accessible. Every product in our range undergoes strict quality control to ensure consistency and potency.

Our B2B pricing model ensures that our trade partners — pharmacies, distributors, and stockists — can maintain healthy margins while offering patients genuinely good herbal alternatives.
